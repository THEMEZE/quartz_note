Chans of small molecules linked together
Each one of the things are called monomers. They have high melting point due to their lengthy structure that lead to strong intermolecular forces.
## Some examples
- Silk
- Biological molecules
	- Cellulose, starch
There are also synthetic ones, like plastic and polystyrene.
