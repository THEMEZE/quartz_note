Matter: Occupies volume and space, particles in constant motion, has mass.
Separation techniques:
- Filtration 
- Dissolution - Solubility
- Crystallization - Heat, cool down to turn into crystals
- Evaporation
- Distillation - Different boiling point
- [[Paper chromatography]] - attraction to mobile phase

# Categorization
Matter can be sorted into two categories, each of which can be separated into two more categories.
Pure: Element, compounds
Mixture: Homo and heterogenous

# States

| Solid                                                                               | Liquid                                                                                 | Gas                                                         |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| High intermolecular forces, particles closely packed. Fixed shape and fixed volume. | Weaker intermolecular forces, particles more spaced. No fixed shape, but fixed volume. | Intermolecular forces negligible, no fixed shape or volume. |

## Changes in state names
Gas $\to$ Liquid: Condensation
Liquid $\to$ Solid: Freezing
Solid $\to$ Gas: Sublimation
Gas $\to$ Solid: Deposition
Solid $\to$ Liquid: Melting
Liquid $\to$ Gas: Vaporizing
